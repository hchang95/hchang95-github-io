changh at berkeley dot edu

I am a 4th year PhD student in the Tsinghua-Berkeley Shenzhen Institute, Tsinghua University co-advised by Prof. Wenwu Zhu and Prof. Somayeh Sojoudi. I received my Bachelor's Degrees in the Department of Electrical Engineering and the School of Economics and Management from Tsinghua University in 2017. In the past, I was also fortunate to work with Prof. Stratos Idreos as a summer intern at DASLab in Harvard in 2016, and with Yu Rong and Prof. Junzhou Huang as a Research Assistant Intern in Tencent AI Lab in 2018, 2020 and 2021.

My research focuses on representation learning, adversarial robustness and machine learning on graph/relational structured data.
